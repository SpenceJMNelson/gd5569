How-to mask a key color:

First, drag a video into your project.

Then, go to Effect > Keying > Color Range

Click the eyedropper and select the background color of your clip

Click the "eyedropper plus" icon to refine your mask

Increasing the "fuzziness" value will select colors close to the selected color

Refine the values until you're satisfied.

Now you can animate your layer using any of the transform controls we learned about last week!
